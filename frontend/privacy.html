Privacy Policy

QuAir is built on a simple principle: your searches are yours. We do not track, profile, or sell your data.

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Information We Do Not Collect

QuAir is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect or store:

  • Your search queries or search history
  • Your IP address or location data
  • Cookies for tracking or profiling purposes
  • Device fingerprints or browser identifiers
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

2. Information We May Collect

To operate and improve QuAir, we may collect limited, anonymized, non-identifying data:

  • Aggregate usage statistics: Total searches per day (no query content)
  • Error logs: Anonymous crash reports to fix technical issues
  • Feedback submissions: Only information you explicitly provide via our feedback form

None of this data can be traced back to an individual user.

3. Cookies

QuAir uses only essential, functional cookies — strictly necessary to operate the site (e.g., session state, preference settings). We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or analytics cookies that identify users across sessions or sites.

4. Third-Party Services

QuAir may use limited third-party infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting, CDN). These partners are contractually prohibited from using any data processed on our behalf for their own purposes. We do not integrate third-party advertising networks or social trackers.

5. Data Security

Since we collect minimal data, our attack surface is minimal. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3+. Feedback form submissions are encrypted at rest. We conduct regular security audits of our infrastructure.

6. Children's Privacy

QuAir does not knowingly collect data from individuals under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through our feedback form, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as QuAir evolves. Any material changes will be announced prominently on this page with an updated date. Continued use of QuAir after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, reach us via our Feedback page. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
