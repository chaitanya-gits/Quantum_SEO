QuAir is a quantum-SEO-powered search engine focused on privacy, relevance, and speed. Unlike traditional search engines optimized for advertising revenue, QuAir ranks results based on genuine signal quality — semantic intent, content accuracy, and contextual relevance.
Quantum SEO refers to a probabilistic, multi-dimensional approach to ranking web content. Instead of linear scoring (like traditional PageRank), QuAir evaluates multiple interpretations of a query simultaneously, weighing semantic context, content freshness, and signal authenticity — reducing the impact of link spam and keyword stuffing.
